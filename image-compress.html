Why Compress Your Images?

Faster Website Loading

Smaller image files load faster, improving your website's performance and user experience. Search engines like Google prioritize fast-loading websites in search results.

Save Storage Space

Compressed images take up less space on your device or server, allowing you to store more photos without upgrading your storage plan.

Better SEO Rankings

Optimized images contribute to better Core Web Vitals scores, which are important ranking factors for search engines.
